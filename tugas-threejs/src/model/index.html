Animaion Control

You can control the animation of the object.

  • Press [W] to activate walk forward

  • Press [A] to activate turn left

  • Press [S] to activate turn walk backward

  • Press [D] to activate turn right

  • Press [SPACE] to activate jump

  • Press [OTHER KEYS] to activate idle

Animaion Control

You can control the camera by choosing one of these

controls and moving your mouse.

  • Left click to move camera

  • Right click to pan camera

  • Zoom to zoom camera
